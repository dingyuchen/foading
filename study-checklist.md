# Study Checklist

## Preview

1. Get a general idea
2. Learn all the terms
3. Watch a summary video
4. Make relevant flashcards
   1. not necessarily applicable
   2. apply the feynman technique here

## View

Focus on the lecture

Think about what kind of question the lecturer would test

## Review

Write down the aforementioned questions.

Make sure you understand

## Test

Maybe 1 day after

Search for practice questions on textbooks or the such

Collect these questions and test regularly (anki?)
